About Us

Our Team

WorldTour Travels Consult is a reputable and well versed organization in the travels and tours industry who have etched their name in the annals of history in the industry by the virtue of quality services we offer to travellers and tourists in the country. WorldTour Travels Consult is a seasoned member of the IATA and have been actively involved in the revolutionization of the industry. We have an assemblage of smart and experienced workforce who are committed in making sure that you have a worthwhile travel experience. With a staff strength of 20, we guarantee on-time delivery for your Visa processing, ticketing, hotel booking, vacation packages, airport pickups, etc. We are recepients of numerous prestigious awards in the travels and tours industry, a testament to our effectiveness which you will affirm once you give us a try.

She is also a graduate of the prestigious Lagos Business School and has done professional courses in some of the best Universities in the world.
